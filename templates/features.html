Club Facilities and Organizer Tools

Everything needed to run team registrations and player rosters smoothly.

Portal Features

  • Create teams with team name and game name.
  • Add players inside teams with age, jersey name, and jersey number.
  • Edit rosters by removing players when needed.
  • Delete teams along with their full player roster.
  • Create tournament events with date ranges.
  • Schedule matches only between teams from the same game.
  • Prevent overlap when a team already has another match in the same game and time range.
  • Use one dashboard to track all teams and players.

Physical Facilities

  • International standard cricket ground with spectator seating.
  • Dedicated football and hockey grounds.
  • Volleyball and handball practice courts.
  • Community gymnasium and wellness zones.
  • Athletic track and conditioning spaces.
